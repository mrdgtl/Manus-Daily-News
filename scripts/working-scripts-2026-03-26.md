## Story 3: Apple and Google Gemini Deal
**Category:** Business
**Source:** The Verge

**[Hook]**
Your next iPhone is going to be powered by Google's AI, but not in the way you think.

**[Context]**
Apple and Google just struck a massive deal. Apple is getting full access to Google's giant Gemini model. But they aren't just plugging Gemini into Siri. They are using a process called "distillation."

**[Significance]**
